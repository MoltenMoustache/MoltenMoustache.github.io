Lost and Found

"You're volunteering at a hospital and your job is to run the Lost and Found booth, returning the lost items of patients who come asking. Make sure to double check before you give away items!"

Lost and Found was made by myself and Jaydon Cueva for the Global Game Jam 2021 (Australia edition). It was developed over the course of two days using the theme 'Lost and Found', my role on the team was the sole programmer.
