Jumpstart Apocalypse

Jumpstart Apocalypse is a stylised VR zombie shooter that was made by a team of 9 developers working remotely over a course of 4 months.

I was Lead Programmer and Gameplay Engineer on the project, on top of my responsibilites to implementing gameplay features, I was responsible for delibirating between the Producer, Designers, Artists and the programming team. Ensuring we meet deadlines and are implementing features according to the design documents.

Contribution

  • Cross-Platform VR through UnityXR
  • Custom VR Input and Interaction System
  • Player controller with smooth locomotion and teleporting
  • Melee, Thrown and Projectile Weapons
  • VR Worldspace UI Interaction
  • Dynamic Inventory System
  • Audio/Particle Pooling System
  • Scripted Events; i.e Bell Tower Explosion
