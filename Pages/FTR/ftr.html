Frame This Redux

Frame This Redux is a quick puzzle game about matching tiles to beat the high score, the game features achievements and online leaderboards and is available on Google Play.

Originally made as a birthday gift, I later renewed the game as I wanted to get some experience with publishing to mobile app stores and intergrating Google Play Game Services.

Development

  • Randomised Goal and Game Boards
  • Touch controls in the form of single and double finger swiping
  • Tile swiping and matching mechanic
  • Timer and Score system
  • Online Leaderboards through Google Play Game Services
  • Achievements through Google Play Game Services
