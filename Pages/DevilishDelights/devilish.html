Devilish Delights

In Devilish Delights, you play as a demon that has to fulfill as many food orders as you can in three minutes in Hell's kitchen. Cook delicious cuisines such as; The Finger Dog, Eyeball Stew, Devilled Eggs

Devilish Delights was made by a team of 10 during a game jam at AIE. The theme was randomly generated for each team and ours was "Blazing Kitchen Project." The team was comprised of 3 designers, 5 artists and 2 programmers. My role on the team was Lead Programmer.

Contribution

  • Player Controller and Bench Interaction
  • Tools for designers to create new recipes and food items
  • Randomised incoming orders
  • Interactable benches for grabbing raw food items and dishes
  • Interactable benches for storing and preparing food
  • Food Delivery and Score System
  • Death/Respawn System
